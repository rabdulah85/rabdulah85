### Hi, I'm Rusli 👋

I'm a doctoral student at **Nagoya University** (GSID), Japan, and a Research Associate at **INDEF Indonesia**. My current work sits at the intersection of econometrics, remote sensing, and regional development in Indonesia — with a focus on **economic development**, **food security**, and **environmental** policy in the Indonesian context.

---

### 🔬 Research Interests

- Spatial econometrics & Exploratory Spatial Data Analysis (ESDA)
- Remote sensing & nighttime lights (NTL) as development proxies
- Causal inference for rural and regional policy evaluation
- Indonesian district-level economics

- Indonesian village-level economics
